Mahatama Gandhi

Father of our Nation

Mahatma Gandhi was born on October 2nd, 1869 in Porbandar, Gujarat. Gandhiji's parents are Karamchand Gandhi and Putlibai Gandhi. Gandhijis wife name is Kasturba Gandhi. Mahatma Gandhi was a freedom fighter. He was a Lawyer, Politician, Activist and a writer. He thought non-violence as a great weapon to the world. He wrote his life in book An autobiography or the story of my experiments with truth. He passed away on January 30, 1948.

You must be the change you wish to see in the world

  • Established the Natal Indian Congress (NIC) in South Africa - 1894
  • Champaran Satyagraha - 1917
  • Kheda Satyagraha - 1918
  • Khilafat Movement - 1919
  • Non-Cooperation Movement - 1920
  • Civil-Disobedience Movement - 1930
  • Quit India Movement - 1942
